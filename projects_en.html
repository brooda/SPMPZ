Meeting of youfth from 8 European countries

Migration Project

Are newcomers European citizens?
¿Los recién llegados son ciudadanos europeos?

For a few years we maintain contacts with group of organisations from seven partner cities, which took on name "7 Cities". Some of them are bound with official partnershim agreements signed by town authorities, other are not formal thus based on will of inhabitants of towns. Our partners are organisations from Bagnols-sur-Ceze in France, Braunfels in Germayny, Carcaixcent in Spain, Eeklo in Belgium, Feltre in Italy, Kiskunfelegyhaza in Hungary i Newbury in England.

Group '7 Cities' (in fact we are 8th member) organises a few thematic meetings each year. Those meetings include meeting of families and youth meetings held in mentioned cities.

At the moment, we are participants of project, which subject is migration (from present perspective as also in the history) in countries of project cities.

Cooperation with Loughborough

Loughborough is a town placed nead Leicester in East Midlands region. Contact with the twinned organisation is held sice first days of operation of our twinning organisation. Our counterpart in Loughborough is group called Friends of Zamość, which is a part the twinning association in Loughborough.

Our contacts are based on yearly meetings of inhabitants of Zamość and Loughborough, organised alternately in both towns. During the meetings guests live in houses of hosts, what has a significant positive effect on making our cooperation more authentic and personal.

Besides, programme of the meeting always contains trips to interesting places. Thanks to it, people from Zamość had a chance to visit places like: Liverpool, York, London, Oxford and Llandudno resort in northern Wales. People from Loughborough sightsaw many cities and regions in Poland, e.g. Gdańsk, Warsaw, Białystok, Cracow and Rzeszów, but also places in neighbouring countries, like: Bardejov, Poprad, Lviv and Vilnius.

In 2017 we hosted English group in Zamość, in 2018 visit od our Association in Loughborough will take place.
