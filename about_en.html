About us

Hello!

We are Zamość's Twinned Towns Association.

Mission of the association is to maintain contacts with our partner towns. We focus on exchanges between inhabitants, language education, promoting Zamość and presenting Polish culture abroad. We carry on activities which subject is European integration.

Association was created in order to make it easier for inhabitants of our town to develop driect contacts with inhabitants of partner towns.

Cooperation with Loghborough in England is the longest for now. Due to this cooperation, every year meeting between inhabitants of two towns take place.

From the beginning of operation, the association was an organizer of many other events, in which counterpart organisations from countries of European Union and Turkey were involved.

Our actions are not only limited to contacts with official partner towns of Zamość. We are open to cooperation with everyone, for whom our city, its history and presence are interesting
