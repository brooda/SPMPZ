Partners

7 cities

For a few years we maintain contacts with group of organisations from seven partner cities, which took on name "7 Cities". Some of them are bound with official partnershim agreements signed by town authorities, other are not formal thus based on will of inhabitants of towns. Our partners are organisations from Bagnols-sur-Ceze in France, Braunfels in Germayny, Carcaixcent in Spain, Eeklo in Belgium, Feltre in Italy, Kiskunfelegyhaza in Hungary amd Newbury in England.

Group '7 Cities' (in fact we are 8th member) organises a few thematic meetings each year. Those meetings include meeting of families and youth meetings held in mentioned cities.

At the moment, we are participants of project, which subject is migration (from present perspective as also in the history) in countries of project cities.
